What does the Accelerator Program cover?

Each training group will be led by an experienced US-qualified accountant (based in the US) with the modules developed based on feedback from accounting firms throughout North America.

Module 1: Working for Agile’s US Team

  1. Standard Agile induction
  2. U.S. culture and history
  3. Communication expectations for U.S. employees
  4. How to WOW your U.S. employer and teammates

Module 2: Bookkeeping in the US

  1. Invoicing, receipting, payment processing
  2. Bank reconciliations and depreciation processing
  3. Balance to Trial Balance and reports
  4. Basic payroll processing
  5. Other ad-hoc duties of a bookkeeper (and how to prepare information ready for an accountant)

Module 3: Accounting in the US

  1. U.S. accounting firms and career progression
  2. U.S. company frameworks and basic business structures (company, partnership, sole trader, joint venture)
  3. U.S. regulations: high-level accounting standards, auditing
  4. Standards and tax laws
  5. Accounting adjustments, prepayments, accruals
  6. Close and reporting: month end, quarter end, half-year, and annual requirements

Module 4: Software Packages used in the US

  1. Take the Introduction to QuickBooks Online
  2. Take and obtain the QuickBooks Online Certification
  3. Take and obtain the QuickBooks Online Advanced Certification
  4. Take the QuickBooks Online Payroll

Module 5: Business Taxes in the US

  1. U.S. tax overview: federal, state, local, and small business taxes – forms, deadlines, and filing
  2. The Internal Revenue Service and how to find information for your clients
  3. Company taxes – EIN, income tax, employment tax, excise tax, property tax, sales tax, and estimated tax
  4. How to prepare business tax information ready for review by a manager
  5. Interview preparation and practice: how to get the role you want and start off on the best foot with your new TOA client/employer

Module 6: Individual Taxes in the US

  1. Taxation for individuals: introduction and overview
  2. Individual taxes in the U.S.: taxation model and what it means
  3. How to prepare individual tax returns ready for review by a manager
  4. Tax recap: individual and business

Module 7: Recap and Next Steps

  1. Recap weeks 1-6, allocation of group presentation projects
  2. 6 group presentations – time for peer review and feedback
  3. Become Xero Advisor Certified and Xero Payroll Certified (if necessary)
  4. Final assessment preparation and completion
  5. Next steps, last-minute questions, and program completion celebration
  6. Interview preparation and practice: how to get the role you want and start off on the best foot with your new Agile client/employer

Qualification and Skills

Qualification and Skills Upon completion of the Accelerator Program, these brilliant accountants will receive an Accounting Foundation Certificate and become a QuickBooks Certified Pro Advisor, plus Xero Certified and Xero Payroll Certified.
